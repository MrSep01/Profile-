About Amelia

Amelia builds classrooms where students ask better questions, write with confidence, and connect literature to real life. Her classes combine discussion-led learning, project-based assessments, and technology-enabled feedback loops.

Teaching Philosophy

  • Every student should leave class with one stronger question.
  • Assessment should show growth, not only final performance.
  • Feedback must be timely, specific, and actionable.
